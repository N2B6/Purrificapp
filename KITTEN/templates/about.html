Read our story

Our Founding

Founded in 2023, Purrific emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize cat adoption in Ireland. Our journey began when a group of passionate cat lovers noticed the growing need for a modern, user-friendly platform that could connect loving homes with cats in need.

Starting with just a handful of partner shelters in Dublin, we've built a community dedicated to responsible pet adoption, education, and support for both cats and their future families.

Growth & Beyond

Since our launch, Purrific has achieved remarkable milestones:

  • Successfully connected over 500 pets with loving homes
  • Partnered with 50+ shelters across Ireland
  • Developed comprehensive adoption screening processes
  • Created educational resources for new cat parents

Looking ahead, we're expanding our services to include post-adoption support, virtual vet consultations, and a nationwide network of cat-loving communities.
